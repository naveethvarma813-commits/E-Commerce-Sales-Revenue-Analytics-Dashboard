# 📊 E-Commerce Sales & Revenue Analytics Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
The **E-Commerce Sales & Revenue Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Excel-based Business Intelligence solution designed to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product revenue contribution, and operational efficiency. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights, enabling businesses to make informed, data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of sales data daily, making it difficult to identify trends, monitor performance, and uncover growth opportunities. This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing a centralized view of key business metrics, helping stakeholders track revenue, customer spending, product performance, and delivery efficiency.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🎉 Occasion-Based Revenue
- Anniversary and Valentine's Day generate the highest revenue.
- Seasonal and festival-based sales significantly impact business performance.

### 🛍️ Category Performance
- Colors, Soft Toys, and Sweets contribute the most revenue.
- Opportunity exists to improve lower-performing categories.

### ⏰ Customer Purchase Behavior
- Higher revenue is generated during afternoon and evening hours.
- Peak shopping times can be leveraged for targeted promotions.

### 📅 Seasonal Trends
- Significant revenue spikes observed during February, August, and November.
- Supports inventory and marketing planning.

### 🌍 Geographic Analysis
- Certain cities consistently contribute higher order volumes.
- Helps identify high-potential markets for expansion.
